You develop a consistent and distinctive brand strategy and visual identity based on target audience analysis.

Inspiration

This is a made up assignment. I wanted to do research on how some of the well-known Dutch artists became famous. I decided that I wanted to search for artists who share a genre simlilar to Noek's. These genres are Dutch pop & sing and songwriter music. In this assignment I described their styles and briefly explain how they got famous.

Reflection

By making this assignment I have learned that artists from the genre Dutch pop/sing and songwriter, each have their own unique style and that most artists became famous after participating in tv-shows.



Target Audience | Strategy - Brand-, Market- and Competitive Analysis

In this assignment I have taken a look at the current state of Noek's brand, analysed the industry in which the artist is active, the competition and her target audience.

Reflection

By making this assignment I have created a full view of Noek's brand, analysed the market so that I now know useful strategies and know who the competition is so that I can adapt my strategy for Noek's branding to be able to outsmart the competition.

Reflection

By making this assignment I have gained a clearer view of my strategy plan.

3. USER EXPERIENCE & USER CENTERED DESIGN

You design prototypes and user research, based on preselected methodologies and on user consults.

User research


Attention!

In the following assignments the aims, results, conclusions, etc. of the tests are in the PDF's, NOT in the reflection or description.

Interview

The aim of the 'Interview' assignment was to see how I can get people to listen to Noek's music. By interviewing several people I also gained a better view of Noek's target audience. I first practiced interviewing with fellow students. After the practice interview I received feedback so that I could improve my questions and the way in which I ask the questions.

What did I learn?

When interviewing, it is important to keep eye contact, body language, being patient and often ask the question 'why?'.

What is my opinion on the assignment?

I think this is a great way to get more information about how others their views on Noek look like.

Would I handle the assignment differently next time?

Next time I would like to interview more people and maybe show the interviewees more material (audio, videos and photos) of Noek.

Task Analysis

For this assignment I made a list of User Stories for the website. This includes the wishes of the artist.

What did I learn?

I learned how to look for small, but important details. For example, "as a user I want to see the item’s name so that I can identify the item". You can probably understand that something like that can easily be forgotten to implement in a website, but is still an important detail.

What is my opinion on the assignment?

I think this assignment was a great way to orientate on how I wanted to make the website for the artist.

Would I handle the assignment differently next time?

Next time, I will not only make a Task Analysis for the website, but also for other media products such as the interactive poster.

Card Sorting

In this assignment I started off making a card sorting for Wekhamp.nl to get a taste on how card sorting works. After the orientation, I made a card sorting for the website of the artist. I made the card sortings with the online tool, optimalworkshop.com

What did I learn?

Card sorting is very easy to do (it takes very little time), but extremely helpful to see what users think like. It shows you what you need to recategorize in order for the user to understand the grouping of the content within the website. Furthermore, I learned how to interpret the data that came out of the results and to make up a conclusion out of this.

What is my opinion on the assignment?

Like the Task Analysis this helped me create a better vision of what the website for the artist should look like.

Would I handle the assignment differently next time?

I would have liked to get a bit more data so next time I will let a few more people take the card sorting test.

User Test

In this assignment I tested the interactive poster prototypes. I first did some research on interactive posters by looking up similar prototypes on the internet. Then I made a preparation document to set goals, research questions, etc. And finally, I tested them with a conclusion as the outcome.

What did I learn?

I learned how to do the Wizard of Oz test. I also learned how to make a scoring system for prototypes. Furthermore, I learned to interpret data to reach a valuable conclusion.

What is my opinion on the assignment?

This is the ideal way to learn how to do tests since this is not just theory, but practice on people that don't know they are being tested.

Would I handle the assignment differently next time?

Next time, I would like to test the prototypes in a different environment for example, at a trainstation.

Expert review

In this assignment I looked at existing products to check if they had usability issues in the form of 'errors'. I also gave a possible solution for these issues ('error prevention')

What did I learn?

I learned how to look at a product from an expert point of view. I also learned how to evaluate a heuristic assignment.

What is my opinion on the assignment?

This is great exercise for when you are reviewing your own finished products. For example, once the website is online you can test if the error alerts you give are given with enough and good information for the user.

Would I handle the assignment differently next time?

No, I think I did it the right way because I followed the guideluines of the famous Nielsen Norman group.

UEX overall

What I really liked about UEX this semester is the User Test assignment since this was practice (praktijk) and not just theory. One thing that was unfortunate is that this is better to do these as a group, but I mostly made UEX assignments individually.

Ik heb me de eerste weken verdiept in de artiest om een goed beeld te krijgen van haar stijl.     Reflectie

    Wat heb ik geleerd?

    Ik heb geleerd duidelijker te communiceren, niet alleen binnen de groep, maar ook met andere groepen (denk aan de photo shoot die niet helemaal soepel verliep).
    Verder heb ik geleerd hoe ik een artiest moet branden. Research doen en contact onderhouden met de artiest zijn hierbij key factors.
    Soms vond ik het onduidelijk wat er gedaan moest worden aangezien de artiest zelf ook niet wist wat ze wilde. Uiteindelijk ben ik erachter gekomen dat je als ICT & Media student met de wensen van de artiest rekening moet houden, maar ook de artiest moet overtuigen van je expertise. Ik heb veel nieuwe tools leren te gebruiken en geleerd feedback te verwerken van opdrachtgevers.

    Wat vond ik van het project?

    Ik had van te voren verwacht dat ieder groepje een andere artiest zou branden. Dit bleek niet zo te zijn. Ik kwam erachter dat het toch handig was dat meerdere groepjes dezeldfe artiest gingen branden, want zo konden we elkaars werk vergelijken.
    Ik vond het erg jammer dat het merendeel van de projectleden weinig motivatie hadden waardoor we niet alle ontwerpen konden realiseren. Ondanks dat het niet altijd mee zat, heb ik toch plezier gehad met dit project. Dit komt omdat ik veel heb mogen experimenteren met originele media producten.

    Wat kan de volgende keer beter?

    Ik vind dat de communicatie binnen de groep en naar andere groepen beter kon. Dit kwam o.a. doordat sommigen projectleden er niet altijd waren en dus weinig meekregen over het project. Een oplossing hiervoor om te voorkomen dat het nog een keer gebeurd, is het sturen van een bericht in Whatsapp. Zo wordt iedereen op de hoogte gebracht en kunnen gesprekken nagelezen worden. Ook moesten sommigen opdrachten met de hele groep gemaakt worden i.p.v. individueel. Hierdoor moesten sommigen meer werk verrichten dan anderen. Dit kwam vooral door de lage motivatie van sommige projectleden. Helaas heb ik de motivatie van deze projectleden niet kunnen verhogen.
